Komi Shouko

Komi San Wa Komyushou Desu (古見さんは、コミュ症です。, こみさんはコミュしょうです。lit. Komi Can't Communicate) is a manga series by Oda Tomohito which began serializing in Weekly Shounen Sunday since November 25, 2016.

A comedy romance written and drawn about girls and boys with problems on their communications to other people. Before the series was published in Weekly Shounen Sunday, it was first published as a one-shot in "Shin Sedai Sunday Grand Prix" in 2015, which resulted in its serialization after gaining popularity from readers.
